How I Can Help

Helping you communicate to your audience through creative design.

Strategy

Every project starts with a simple questionnaire and a discovery session. With this we can begin to clarify your business’ web goals, figure out what’s working and not with your current site, and collaborate to develop the optimal site content.

Design

This is where the fun begins! I’ll design a user-friendly website that will help attract your ideal clients. Along the way I’ll create mockups of the entire website so you could get a sense of how it will look and function before it even goes live.

Development

Once the design and the content is locked down, I’ll create a fully responsive website that looks and functions great on all devices. I will walk you through the details of your new site and answer any questions that may arise.

Support

Building a business and brand never stops so I’ll continue to be there for you now and in the future with ongoing support. Whether you need content updates, new site pages or some design advice I’ll be there for you.
