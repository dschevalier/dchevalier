GoodBowl

In my free time I like to explore ideas and concepts in my head and this one happens to center around healthy eatery. I started to work up what I would envision as the landing page for the business, in both desktop and mobile views.

I imagine carrying the design forward with generous amounts of spacing to the sections and large scaled images. I would want the focus of the website to be appealing images of food with bare amounts of needed copy. It’s my intention to one day revisit this concept and see where it evolves.
