Hello, I'm happy you’re here!

I've always loved spending way too much time on the computer, and spent a fair amount of my youth tinkering with code and designing crappy websites for myself. At the time, I felt unable to follow this passion fully into a career so I decided to pursue other paths as safer ventures for my future.

Yet as it turns out, my path would turn full circle when I was privileged enough to switch careers away from business management completely and focus on becoming a freelance designer. Though it has not always been an easiest direction, I have found it rewarding to work with individuals in finding their vision and voice for the web.

This adventure would not be possible without all the love and support of my badass partner Jules, whom I share a home with in Western Massachusetts. This home is also occupied by three wonderful furballs, Lucius, Mr. Gizmo and General Grievous.

As far as off-hour activities go, you'll probably find me hunting for Star Wars action figures, napping somewhere comfortable, or trying to find the best way to organize something.
